Return Policy

Effective Date: May 23, 2025

Returns & Exchanges

At Home Decor Outlet, we want you to love your purchase. If you're not completely satisfied, we offer returns within 14 days of your purchase or delivery date for eligible products.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Items must be returned within 14 days of the purchase or delivery date.
  • Products must be unused, undamaged, and in original packaging with tags intact.
  • Receipt or proof of purchase is required.

Non-Returnable Items

These items are final sale and not eligible for return:

  • Custom or made-to-order products
  • Clearance items marked as final sale
  • Opened or used bedding, linens, or personal items

Return Instructions

Items may be returned in-store or by mail. For mail returns, contact our customer care at support@homedecoroutlet.com for a return authorization and instructions.

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the product was received damaged or incorrect.

Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we’ll send you a notification. Approved refunds will be issued to your original payment method within 5–7 business days.

Exchanges

We only replace items if they’re defective or damaged. To request an exchange, email us at support@homedecoroutlet.com with your order number and photos, if applicable.
